Inter-College Maths and Physics Quiz
On October 15, 2018, the Applied Physics Club and Domain (Math Club) organized an inter-college Maths and Physics Quiz competition. The event aimed to promote academic excellence, enhance critical thinking and foster healthy competition among students. The event was a huge success, with participation from 36 teams, including nine teams from other colleges.
The competition was divided into three rounds: the preliminary round, the semi-finals, and the finals. The teams that made it through the preliminary round were qualified for the semi-finals. The competition was intense, with each team vying for the top spot.
The final round of the competition was exhilarating, with the top three teams competing fiercely for the top position. After a tough fight, the winning team was announced, and they were rewarded with trophies, prizes, and certificates. The runners-up and the third-placed team were also recognized with certificates and prizes.
The event was a great opportunity for students to showcase their knowledge and skills in Maths and Physics. It was a great platform for students to learn, grow and network with their peers from different colleges. The event was well-organized, and the participants had a great time.
In conclusion, the inter-college Maths and Physics Quiz competition was a resounding success. It was a great platform for students to showcase their academic prowess, learn, and grow. The organizers deserve commendation for putting together such a fantastic event. The event should be continued in future years to foster healthy competition among students and promote academic excellence.
