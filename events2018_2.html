Intra College Maths and Physics Quiz
The Math-Physics quiz was organized by the Math and Physics club of DBIT on 3rd October 2018. The event was a huge success, with students from all departments participating in the quiz. The event was conducted by the Domain and the Physics club volunteers.
The quiz had two rounds. For the first round, the teams were given half an hour to solve the questions. The questions were challenging, and the teams had to put in a lot of effort to solve them. The top 8 teams from round 1 qualified for the second round.
The second round commenced with the top 8 teams from round 1. The teams were asked more challenging questions, and they had to use their knowledge and logical reasoning skills to answer them. The competition was tough, and the teams had to put in a lot of effort to stay ahead of their opponents.
The event was well-organized, and the participants had a great time. The quiz was a great opportunity for the students to showcase their knowledge and skills in Mathematics and Physics. The event was a great success, and the Math and Physics club of DBIT received a lot of praise for organizing such a wonderful event.
