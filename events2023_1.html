Escape Room 2023 (Colosseum DBIT)10 am to 05 pm
The Escape Room event with the theme of solving a murder mystery was a thrilling experience that kept everyone on the edge of their seats. The organizers went all out to create an immersive environment, complete with horror stories and twisted plotlines that kept the adrenaline pumping.
As soon as the participants entered the room, they were transported to a world of mystery and intrigue. The dimly lit room was filled with clues and hidden messages that needed to be deciphered in order to solve the crime. The participants were given a set of riddles and puzzles that they had to solve to unlock the clues, and as they progressed, the tension mounted.
The horror stories that were woven into the narrative added an extra layer of suspense to the event. From creaking doors to eerie sounds, the atmosphere was designed to keep the participants on edge. The twisted plotlines were also a highlight of the event, with unexpected twists and turns that kept the participants guessing until the very end.
Overall, the Escape Room event was a great success. It was a unique and modern way to engage people in a murder mystery and provided an opportunity for people to test their problem-solving skills in a fun and exciting way. The event was a reminder that solving a mystery is not just about finding the answer, but also about the journey and the experience that comes with it.
