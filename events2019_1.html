InQUIZitive 2019
The Maths and Physics club of Don Bosco Institute of Technology (DBIT), in collaboration, organized a Math and Physics Inter-Collegiate Quiz Contest on 21st February 2019, during the academic year. The event was named InQUIZitive 2019 and aimed to encourage and challenge the students' knowledge in the subjects of mathematics and physics.
Several teams from other colleges participated in the event, and the teams had to go through two rounds to make it to the finals. The questions in the rounds were designed to test the participants' understanding, analytical skills, and logical reasoning abilities.
The quiz masters for the event were the alumni of DBIT, who were well-versed with the subjects and the quiz format. The event was conducted in a professional manner, and the participants showed great enthusiasm and sportsmanship.
The winners of the event were appreciated with prizes and certificates. The prizes were given away by the faculty members of the Maths and Physics club of DBIT. The winners were applauded for their efforts and knowledge in the subjects, and the certificates were handed over to them as a token of recognition.
Overall, the event was a great success, and it provided a platform for the students to showcase their knowledge and skills. The event also promoted healthy competition and encouraged the students to take an interest in the subjects of mathematics and physics. The event helped in the overall development of the students and was a memorable experience for all those who participated.
