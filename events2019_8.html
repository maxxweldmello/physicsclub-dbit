Fizzics

Introduction:


Fizzics, a science fest based on physics, was held on 18th February 2019 at RD National College, Bandra, Mumbai. The event was organized by the college's Physics Department and aimed to provide a platform for students to showcase their scientific projects and gain exposure to the latest developments in the field of physics. The event featured impressive exhibitions, project presentations, and fun activities, and was attended by students, teachers, and professionals from the physics community.

Exhibitions and Project Presentations:


The event featured an impressive array of exhibitions and project presentations that demonstrated the latest advances in the field of physics. The students from DBIT (Don Bosco Institute of Technology) showcased a few projects, including Ultrasonic Object Detector, Van de Graff Generator, Li-Fi System, Reverb Amplifier, and Activated Charcoal for Water Purification. These projects showcased the students' innovation and technical skills, and were well-received by the attendees.
Dr. R. D. Mishra, the chief guest of the event, delivered talks on Optics, Digital image processing, Space Rovers, and Magic of Numbers. His talks were informative and engaging, and provided many interesting insights into the latest developments in the field of physics. Dr. Mishra's talks were well-received by the attendees, and provided an opportunity for students and professionals to learn from an expert in the field.
