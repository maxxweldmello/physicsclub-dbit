INTERACTION OF EXTC STUDENTS WITH DR. DHANANJAY PATE

Introduction

On 21 February 2019, an event was organized at the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Don Bosco Institute of Technology (DBIT), Mumbai, India. The event was named "Interaction of EXTC Students with Dr. Dhananjay Patel." Dr. Patel is a professor at DBIT and a PhD holder from IIT Bombay. He shared his research journey in Fiber Optics and inspired the students to pursue their curiosity.

Events Details

The event started at 10:00 AM and was attended by the students of the Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering department. Dr. Patel started the session by introducing himself and his research work in Fiber Optics. He explained the basics of Fiber Optics and how it has revolutionized the field of telecommunication and data transmission.
Dr. Patel also shared his personal experience of pursuing a PhD from IIT Bombay and how it helped him to develop a deeper understanding of the subject. He emphasized the importance of research and how it can lead to groundbreaking discoveries in any field.
Dr. Patel also showcased some of the brilliant applications of optical fibers in various fields such as telecommunication, medicine, and defense. He explained how optical fibers can be used to transmit large amounts of data over long distances without any loss of signal quality. He also demonstrated how optical fibers are used in medical imaging to diagnose and treat diseases.
Dr. Patel also encouraged the students to pursue their curiosity and explore the field of Fiber Optics. He urged them to ask questions and seek answers to satisfy their curiosity. He also shared his personal journey of pursuing his passion for research and how it led him to where he is today.

Conclusion:

The "Interaction of EXTC Students with Dr. Dhananjay Patel" was an informative and inspiring event. Dr. Patel's knowledge and experience in the field of Fiber Optics were evident in his presentation. The event encouraged the students to pursue their curiosity and explore the field of Fiber Optics further. It was a valuable learning experience for the students and helped them to gain insights into the field of Fiber Optics.
