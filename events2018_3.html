Poster Making Compition
The Physics Club of DBIT organized a Physics Poster design competition in the year 2018. A total of 11 teams participated of which 3 teams posters came out to be the best among others. The posters were judged on various parameters by the faculty members. Our Principal, Dr. Prasanna madam gave away the prizes. A few posters are displayed on the notice boards and also in the Physics lab of DBIT.
